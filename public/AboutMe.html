About Me

My name is Kevin Tse (pronounced "see"), and I am a sophomore in college in the Suburbs of Chicago, IL. I am an avid photographer and enjoy traveling, playing soccer, and running. I began taking pictures 4 years ago for a documentary about life in a city and have continued to expand my subjects to include landscapes, sunsets, portraits, and sports photography. Since then, I have taken and edited hundreds of photos that have been featured in local showcases. I plan to continue my photography in college and beyond.



Specifics:

  • I shoot with a Nikon DSLR D7200
  • I edit with Photoshop CC and Lightroom CC
  • I have experience taking personal and group portraits, structural landscapes, and natural scenery. Additionally, I enjoy taking time-lapses
  • My favorite subjects include architectural structures and group portaits
